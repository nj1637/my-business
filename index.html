Privacy Policy
My Business ERP · Last updated March 2026
📋 By using My Business ERP, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Data We Collect

We collect business data you enter including invoice details, client information, product inventory, expenses, and business settings. This data is stored securely in your Firebase account and locally on your device.

2. How Your Data is Used

Your data is used exclusively to provide ERP functionality — generating invoices, tracking inventory, managing clients, and producing business reports. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in your personal Firebase Firestore database. Authentication is handled by Firebase Auth (Google). Data is also cached locally (localStorage/IndexedDB) for offline access. Your data is isolated to your account UID and not accessible by other users.

4. Employee Data

When you add employees, their login credentials (email/password) are stored locally on the device. Employee activity logs are stored locally and are only visible to the account owner. Employees access the same business data as the owner, restricted by permissions you set.

5. Third-Party Services

This app uses Firebase (Google) for authentication and database storage, Tailwind CSS for styling, Chart.js for charts, and html2pdf.js for PDF generation. No data is shared with these services beyond what is necessary for their function.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

We use localStorage and IndexedDB to store your data locally for offline access and session persistence. No tracking cookies are used.

7. Your Rights

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting support or deleting your Firebase project. You have full ownership of your business data.

8. Contact

1 Create Your First Invoice

Go to Invoice tab → Enter client name → Search & add products → Choose payment mode (UPI/Cash/Credit) → Click Preview & Finalize → Confirm to save.

2 Add Products to Inventory

Go to Inventory → Click Add → Fill product name, category, HSN, rate, GST%, and opening stock → Save. Products appear in invoice search automatically.

3 Add Services

Go to Services → Click Add Service → Fill name, category, duration, rate → Save. In Invoice, click ✨ Services toggle to search & add services.

4 Add Employees

Go to Employees → Click Add Employee → Fill name, department → Go to Login tab, set email & password → Go to Role & Access tab, choose a template (Billing Staff / Receptionist) or set individual permissions → Save.

5 Send Invoice via WhatsApp

After finalizing, click View on the invoice from Records → PDF Preview opens → Click green WhatsApp button → PDF attaches automatically on Android.

6 Digital QR on Invoice

Every finalized invoice has a Scan for Digital Copy QR code. Client scans it with any phone camera → Opens the exact same invoice in browser → Can print or save as PDF.

7 Business Settings

Go to Settings → Set business name, GSTIN, address, bank details, UPI ID, and upload logo & signature. These appear on all invoices automatically.

8 Recurring Invoices

Sidebar → Recurring → Set client, amount, frequency (weekly/monthly) → Due date pe "Generate" click karo → Invoice pre-filled hoga → Finalize karo.
